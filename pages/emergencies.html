Emergencies

If your child is experiencing a life threatening emergency, please call 911 or head to your nearest Emergency Room.

We are available 24 hours a day. If your child is ill or injured when our office is not open, please call 617-232-2811. There is a physician on call who can provide medical advice for urgent issues that cannot wait until the office opens. The on call physician will call you back within an hour. If your concern cannot wait 60-90 minutes, please indicate that to our answering service and the on call physician will call you back as soon as possible.
